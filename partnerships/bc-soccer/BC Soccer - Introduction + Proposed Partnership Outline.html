01 · Partnership · BC Soccer founding data partner

A founding partnership to build the GK-aware AI that doesn't exist yet.

StixAnalytix is asking BC Soccer to help train the next generation of goalkeeper-specific match analytics on real Canadian youth footage — under a privacy framework BC Soccer audits, and a partnership structure that puts the federation at the leading edge of goalkeeper development and analysis in Canada.

The opportunity

Goalkeeping is the most-coached, least-data-supported position in soccer. Today's video AI treats the GK as background — it counts goals on a scoreboard but misses the save, the distribution, the read. We're building the model that doesn't. To make it work on the footage that actually matters — youth and academy matches with no broadcast scoreboard, mixed camera angles, real-world quality — we need to train on real Canadian youth match video. BC Soccer is the natural founding partner: the largest GK development pipeline in the province, and the federation best-placed to shape what this model becomes for every keeper who follows.

What we need

  • 200+ full game MP4s of competitive youth match footage — BCSPL, academy, or member-club games where a primary GK is identifiable.

Privacy — non-negotiable

Three commitments, written into the partnership agreement before any footage moves:

  • Training use only. The corpus exists to train the StixAnalytix GK model and for BC Soccer's own coach education — nothing else. Never used in product demos, sales material, or external presentations. Any demo we run uses a separately-consented goalkeeper with explicit permission to share their clips and data.
  • BC Soccer access via dedicated login. Clips are labeled with the keeper's name so your coaches can identify whose play they're studying. No third-party access, no resale, no other product lines.
  • BC Soccer audit and deletion rights. Full visibility into every clip in the corpus throughout the partnership. Irreversible deletion on BC Soccer's written request, at any time.

What BC Soccer gets

Founding
Partner designation. Co-branding and co-marketing shaped together. Leading edge of GK development in Canada.
Library
Full access to the training-clip library, labeled by keeper — ready-to-use teaching material for BC Soccer coach education.
$30K · 1 yr
Free platform usage for new BC Soccer uploads — triggered the moment the model reaches 90% accuracy.

The trigger

90% accuracy isn't a marketing target — it's the trigger event. The day the model crosses that threshold on Canadian youth match footage, BC Soccer's free-usage year begins and the model deploys into the platform your keepers will use. Until then, the value to BC Soccer is the founding-partner positioning, full access to the anonymized clip library for coach education, and a seat at the table while the model gets built. The pipeline built here becomes the template for every other Canadian province. BC Soccer's name goes on it first.
