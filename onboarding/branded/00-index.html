02 · Two tracks · choose yours

Two readers. Two paths through the package.

The full package is built for two audiences with different goals. Pick the path that fits.

Volunteer labeler track

You're going to label real matches. The work matters because every match you process becomes training data.

  1. Read 01 → 02 in full.
  2. Skim 03a — get oriented to the sheets.
  3. Read 04 Tool Walkthrough.
  4. Join the calibration cohort (see 05).
  5. Once calibrated, work daily from 03a + 03b.

Once calibrated, you're typically labeling 5-10 matches per week.

ML team / technical partner track
You're evaluating the system, not labeling matches. Goal: understand how clean the training data is. Start 01 Mission & Why — strategic context. Then 02 Domain Primer (skim) — to understand the vocabulary. Then 03a Labeling Rubric — the contract between human labelers and the model. Finally 05 Calibration Process — how we generate clean data at scale.
